########### ABOUT ############

This script exports a Pivotal Tracker to a PDF suitable for printing and cutting into notecards. Its based on

- the gist at https://gist.github.com/405984/ffd3b9e6c391b8366048fe3c7b0b839335a22b8a
- http://github.com/brynary/features2cards/
- http://ephemera.karmi.cz/post/622136360/create-printable-pdf-cards-for-your-pivotal-tracker-stor


########### USAGE ############
ruby pivotal_csv_to_pdf_cards.rb [PATH TO YOUR CSV]
for example, "ruby pivotal_csv_to_pdf_cards.rb ~/Downloads/my_project_20110816_1959.csv"
